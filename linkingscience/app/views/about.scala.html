About the website and how to contact us

The Website

The purpose of this website is to increase the speed of scientific projects. We are conscious of the power of computer science and all the potential help it could provide to different scientific fields. Unfortunately we are also aware of the fact that computer science is evolving quickly and is not always easy to understand and manipulate.

This website offer the possibility to everyone who has an idea of a project that could be useful to improve our world to host it on the plateform. The goal is to attract scientists and software creators to realize these projects. Share knowledge and create a better world. We are providing everything we can to make all of this a succes.
